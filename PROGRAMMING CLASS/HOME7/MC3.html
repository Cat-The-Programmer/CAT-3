Coal

Coal ore is a mineral block that drops coal when mined and the deepslate version of itself is a variant of coal ore that can generate in deepslate and tuff blobs. Coal ore can generate in the Overworld in the form of blobs. Coal ore attempts to generate 20 times per chunk in blobs of size 0-37, from levels 0 to 127, in all biomes. An average of 207 coal ore generates per chunk. Coal ore can replace stone, granite, diorite, andesite, tuff, and deepslate. Coal ore that replaces tuff or deepslate will become deepslate coal ore.

Iron

Iron ore is a mineral block found underground. It is a source of raw iron, which can be smelted into iron ingots. The deepslate version of itself is a variant of iron ore that can generate in deepslate and tuff blobs. Iron ore itself can be obtained by mining it with a stone pickaxe or higher enchanted with Silk Touch. When mined without Silk Touch, iron ore drops raw iron. It is affected by Fortune enchantment, dropping 1–2, 1–3, or 1–4 raw iron respectively with Fortune I, II, and III.

Copper

Copper ore is a mineral block found underground. The primary usage of copper ore is to obtain copper ingots. Nine copper ingots can be crafted into copper blocks. Copper ore does not weather like its block counterpart, though the ore itself shows some oxidization. Copper ore must be mined with a stone pickaxe or higher, or else it drops nothing.

Gold

Gold ore is a rare mineral block found underground. The deepslate version of itself is a variant of gold ore that can generate in deepslate and tuff blobs. Piglins become hostile toward players who mine gold ore, even if the player is wearing golden armor. Piglins also run toward any gold ores on the ground, and inspect it for 6 to 8 seconds before putting it in their inventory.

Lapis Lazuli

Lapis lazuli ore is the ore block from which lapis lazuli is obtained. The deepslate version of itself is a variant of lapis lazuli ore that can generate in deepslate and tuff blobs. Lapis lazuli is a mineral used for enchanting and decoration.

Redstone

Redstone ore is the ore block from which redstone is obtained. The deepslate version of itself is a variant of redstone ore that can generate in deepslate and tuff blobs. Redstone dust is a mineral that can transmit redstone power when placed as a block. Redstone dust is used for brewing, crafting, and in redstone circuits by placing it on the ground to create redstone wire. It can also be used to power redstone components.

Diamond

Diamond ore is a rare ore that generates deep underground, and is the only reliable source of diamonds. The deepslate version of itself is a variant of diamond ore that can generate in deepslate and tuff blobs. Diamonds are mainly used to craft high-tier tools and armor, enchanting tables, blocks of diamond, and jukeboxes.

Emerald

Emerald ore is a rare mineral block that generates only in mountains biomes. It drops emeralds when mined, or itself if mined with a pickaxe with the Silk Touch enchantment. The deepslate version of itself is a variant of emerald ore that can generate in deepslate and tuff blobs. They are rare minerals that are used primarily as the currency for trading with villagers and wandering traders. Naturally-occurring emeralds are rarer than diamonds.
